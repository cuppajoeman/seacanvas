About the Artist

I live on the Bay of Fundy, Nova Scotia. The tides here are the highest in the world, and their arrival and receding create ethereal seascapes on vast palettes of clay. I never tire of the changing views where sea meets earth and sky, exquisitely beautiful no matter the time of day or year.
